Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and scheduling.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes. If you call in the morning, we can often provide same-day pickup anywhere in the Bay Area.

How do you price junk removal?

Pricing is primarily based on volume (how much space your items take in our truck), item type, and access. We always provide a no-obligation on-site quote before any work begins.

Which items can you take?

Most non-hazardous items including furniture, appliances, yard waste, construction debris, and electronics. For hazardous materials, please call for guidance.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for your protection.

What areas do you serve?

Alameda, Contra Costa, San Mateo, Santa Clara, San Francisco, and surrounding areas.
